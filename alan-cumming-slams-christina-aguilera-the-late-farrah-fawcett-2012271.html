Alan Cumming Slams Christina Aguilera, the Late Farrah Fawcett

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Andy Cohen has put a myriad of celebrities in the hot seat while playing "Plead the Fifth" on Watch What Happens Live, eliciting candid revelations from even the most private stars.

PHOTOS: Choose sides in these 2011 celebrity feuds

On Thursday's episode of the Bravo show, actor Alan Cumming, 47, was put to the test when Cohen, 43, asked him whether Cher or Christina Aguilera was the bigger diva on the set of Burlesque.

Without hesitation, Cumming replied: "Christina." (The all-star cast also featured the talents of Julianne Hough, Kristen Bell, Cam Gigandet, Stanley Tucci, Peter Gallagher and Dianna Agron.)

Cohen then asked Cumming: "What TV or film star needs to never grace the Broadway stage again?" The actor — married to Grant Shaffer since 2007 — paused for a moment before offering an answer. "Farrah Fawcett," he finally said.

Fawcett, who lost her two-year battle with cancer in June 2009, earned decent reviews for her off-Broadway performance in Extremities. Her official Broadway debut was canceled in late 2003 while still in previews.
