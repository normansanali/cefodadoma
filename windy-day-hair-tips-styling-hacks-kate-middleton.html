Publish date: 2024-07-12

Switch Up Your Style: A little bit of windblown texture looks cool, but the easiest way to avoid a completely crazy windswept look is to opt for an updo — be it a sleek ponytail or messy side braid.

If you like the volumized blowout look, consider following your usual routine and then combing your hair back into a tight high ponytail whose ends still have voluminous, loose curls. If a more relaxed look is more your speed, style your hair into a slightly messy side braid, working a bit of dry shampoo like Verb‘s into your roots and sprinkled throughout your braid for more body and grip.

Windy Day Hair Tips — How To Avoid A Bad Hair Day During Extreme Weather

On-The-Go Essentials: If, like Kate, you stand by your blowout no matter what Mother Nature throws your way, be prepared to have to touch up your look a bit. Try to tuck as much of your hair as you can into your coat or even under a beanie (just make sure it’s not too form-fitting, or you’ll end up with hat hair). Once you get to your destination, keep a mini comb like The Sephora Collection Mini Tidy Detangling Comb in your purse to brush through knots without ruining the style.
