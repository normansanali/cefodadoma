Linda Hogan is an American television personality. She is widely known for her role on the American reality television show Hogan Knows Best.

Linda Hogan Husband

Hulk Hogan is the ex-husband of Linda Hogan. Hulk Hogan is an American retired professional wrestler and television personality.

Linda Hogan Net Worth

The reality show star, public speaker and entrepreneur, Linda Hogan has a net worth of $20 million.

Linda Hogan Wedding

Linda and Hulk married on December 18, 1983. They were together for 26 years and had two children, Brooke Hogan and Nick Hogan.

Linda Hogan Young

In 2008, 48-year-old Linda dated 19-year-old Charley Hill. They got engaged a year later but broke it off in 2012.

Linda Hogan Now 2022

In recent years, Linda has kept away from the spotlight, even though she is active on Twitter. On her bio on the microblogging site, Linda describes herself as an animal rights activist and often shares posts promoting animal issues.
