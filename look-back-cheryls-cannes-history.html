A Look Back At Cheryl's Cannes History

Publish date: 2024-07-25

As the build up to the 2016 Cannes Film Festival gets under way we take a look back at the gorgeous dresses worn by Cheryl as she walked the famous red carpet.

Since becoming a L’Oreal Paris UK ambassador back in 2009, Cheryl has walked the red carpet at the Cannes Film Festival every year.

Cheryl made her debut at the prestigious festival way back in 2010 and will once again be attending the event this year.

The Cannes Film Festival will start on May 11th and will be attended by the former X Factor judge and a host of other big names.

To celebrate the Cannes red carpet, we here at Celeb Mix have decided  to take a look back at Cheryl’s gorgeous Cannes red carpet history.

In 2010 making her debut at the glamorous event, Cheryl wore a stunning white cut-away Versace gown and wore her hair in loose waves.

in 2011 the sexy singer one again walked the red carpet this time wearing a gorgeous plunging Stephane Rolland white dress with her hair in a sleek pony.

The 2012 Cannes Film Festival saw Cheryl wearing a white Stephane Rolland dress once again, but this time the stunning gown had a dramatic red feathered train, the singer wore her hair loose.

2013 saw the brunette beauty wearing a stunning red Zuhair Murad sequinned dress with her hair tied back in an intricate plait.

2014 saw Chezza opting for a Gothic black Monique Lhuillier gown with her hair tied back in a messy low bun.

Last year saw the ‘Call My Name’ hitmaker opt for a stunning dusky pink Ralph and Russo gown and wore her hair in a chic up-do.
