Career

Based in Akron, Ohio, he is a sculptor, artist, and master craftsman. He was born in Warren, Ohio, and graduated from Kent State University with a bachelor’s degree in art. He mostly works with metals, such as aluminum, steel, and pewter.

Don Drumm has won several awards and distinctions, including the Ohio Designer Crafts “Lifetime Achievement Award” and the Akron Beacon Journal Newspaper’s “Outstanding Contributors of the Century.” In 2000, he was the first recipient of both the Akron Area Arts Alliance’s Outstanding Visual Artist Award and the American Institute of Architecture’s (AIA) “Artist and Craftsman Excellence” award.
