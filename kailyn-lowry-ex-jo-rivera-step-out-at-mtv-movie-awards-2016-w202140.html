Kailyn Lowry, Ex Jo Rivera Step Out at MTV Movie Awards 2016

Publish date: 2024-09-25

Um, what’s happening? Kailyn Lowry stepped out with ex-boyfriend Jo Rivera at the 2016 MTV Movie Awards in Burbank, California, on Saturday, April 9, sending Teen Mom 2 fans into a frenzy.

The reality star, 24, showed off her stunning new figure in a low-cut black dress almost three months after she underwent extensive plastic surgery in January. Rivera, who is the father of Lowry’s son Isaac, 6, wore a khaki suit and black shirt.

Lowry was also notably spotted without a wedding ring on her finger (she is currently married to Javi Marroquin).

The exes recently got into an epic fight, which Lowry secretly filmed on her cellphone, during the Monday, April 4, episode of Teen Mom 2. Lowry showed up 30 minutes early to pick up Issac and insulted her ex’s sweatpants, telling him, “[It’s] 4:30 on a Sunday. You should have your life together.”

Rivera told Us on the red carpet at the MTV Movie Awards on Saturday that the pair were “just hanging out” at the event. “We’re doing great,” he added. “We’re maintaining a good friendship.”

Earlier this week, Rivera announced that he and Vee Torres are engaged. The couple welcomed their first child together, Velisse, last October.
