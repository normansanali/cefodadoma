Publish date: 2024-09-28

Grio Awards 2023

The 2023 Byron Allen Presents TheGrio Awards took place on Saturday, November 25, honoring Black excellence across various fields like film, television, science, journalism, comedy, and environmental activism. The annual national awards, organized by TheGrio, a prominent American news website focused on African Americans, aim to recognize outstanding achievements in a range of categories. The Grio Awards have become a significant platform for acknowledging and appreciating contributions from individuals who are shaping America’s future. Started in 2010, the awards have evolved to encompass diverse areas of influence, reflecting the broad impact of the honorees on the nation.

The Grio Awards consist of ten distinct categories, including Business, Health, Education, Science & the Environment, Media, Pop Culture, Arts & Culture, Service & Activism, Politics, and Sports. In each category, ten individuals are carefully selected based on their notable contributions. To be featured on The Grio’s 100 list, awardees must demonstrate a substantial and ongoing impact on America as a whole, reaching beyond the boundaries of the African American community. The editorial team at theGrio.com, in collaboration with contributors and experts, plays a crucial role in determining the final list of award winners, ensuring a comprehensive representation of excellence across various sectors.

Grio Awards 2023 Highlights

The Grio Awards 2023 showcased spectacular highlights and unforgettable moments to celebrate the honorees’ excellence. Mariah Carey, crowned the “Queen of Christmas,” was bestowed with the Music Icon Award, and her tribute was nothing short of extraordinary. Patti LaBelle, Jennifer Hudson, Coco Jones, and Boyz II Men joined forces to pay homage to Carey, with Coco impressively tackling the vocal challenges of their classic collaboration, “One Sweet Day.”

Grio Awards 2023 Hosts

The dynamic hosting duo of The Grio Awards 2023 featured Sheryl Underwood, co-host of The Talk, and the talented comedian Roy Wood Jr. Together, they took the reins as hosts for the two-hour special, guiding the audience through a celebration of “icons, leaders, and legends” within the African-American community. Their charismatic and engaging presence set the tone for an evening that honored individuals across a spectrum of categories, including film, music, comedy, television, sports, philanthropy, business, fashion, social justice, environmental justice, and education.

Grio Awards 2023 Creator

The visionary behind The Grio Awards 2023 is none other than Byron Allen, the founder, chairman, and CEO of Allen Media Group. As the driving force behind the African-American-focused news, lifestyle, sports, and entertainment platform, theGrio, Allen created the Grio Awards to serve as a platform for celebrating and amplifying African American excellence. With a deep understanding of the impact strong and positive African American icons had on his own life, Allen emphasizes the importance of recognizing and honoring iconic individuals who have made significant contributions to their communities.

How to Watch Grio Awards 2023?

To catch The Grio Awards 2023, which aired on CBS on Saturday, November 25, viewers had the option to stream the event on Paramount+. The two-hour show commenced at 8 p.m. ET, offering an opportunity for audiences to witness the celebration of African-American excellence.

What are the Grio Awards?

The Grio Awards are an annual national recognition ceremony presented by TheGrio, an American news website focused on African Americans. Created by Byron Allen, founder of Allen Media Group, the awards celebrate excellence in categories such as film, television, science, journalism, comedy, environmental activism, and more. Winners are selected from diverse fields, with a commitment to honoring those who shape America’s future. The event features ten award categories spanning Business, Health, Education, Science & the Environment, Media, Pop Culture, Arts & Culture, Service & Activism, Politics, and Sports. The honorees, chosen by theGrio.com’s editorial team, must demonstrate significant impact and ongoing contributions to America.
