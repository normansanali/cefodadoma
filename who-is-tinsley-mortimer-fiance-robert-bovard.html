Who is Tinsley Mortimer Fiance, Robert Bovard?

Publish date: 2024-09-02

American socialite and television personality Tinsley Randolph Mortimer, 48, was born in the United States. She gained notoriety as the star of The Real Housewives of New York City and High Society, two reality television programs.

Who Is Tinsley Mortimer?

In Richmond, Virginia, Tinsley Randolph Mercer gave birth to Mortimer. Her mother, Dale Tatum Mercer, is an interior designer and socialite, and her father, George Riley Mercer Jr., was an investor in real estate.

Dabney Winston Mercer is her younger sister. In 1936, George Riley Mercer Sr., her paternal grandpa, established Mercer Rug Cleansing. Mortimer was raised at Graymont, her family’s Richmond home, and is descended from the First Families of Virginia.

Tinsley Mortimer Career

Mortimer began her career as an event planner for Harrison & Shriftman and as a beauty assistant at Vogue.

Samantha Thavasa was the subject of an advertisement campaign for Mortimer’s handbag and clothing collection in Japan.

The “Samantha Thavasa by Tinsley Mortimer” handbag line had its American premiere in winter 2006, with its flagship location in New York City.

She was also heavily highlighted in the Madison Avenue store of Samantha Thavasa USA, which sells her handbags, as well as in the company’s web promotional materials. Later on, she started her own clothing brand called Riccime by Tinsley Mortimer, which was only available in Japan.

Mortimer started working on the branding and design of a condominium project in 2008.

In addition to being a Christian Dior beauty spokesperson, Mortimer was involved in the creation of the lip gloss Tinsley Pink.

She has been featured in various editorials for magazines, such as Marie Claire, Harper’s Bazaar, Vogue, and WWD.

She starred on the May 2012 cover of Vestal magazine, which also included an editorial by fashion photographer Kevin Sinclair and styled by Lorenzo Hill-White. She has been featured on the covers of several magazines, including New York, Avenue, CVLUX, Maniac, Virginia Living, and Page Six Magazine.

In her book Southern Charm, written by Mortimer, the reader follows Minty Davenport as she travels from South Carolina to New York, where she becomes the target of gossip.

April 2013 saw Simon & Schuster publish it.

Mortimer also authored a chapter in Manhattan diet physician Stuart Fischer’s book The Park Avenue Diet, which offers advice on enhancing social and conversational abilities. Since 2017, she has contributed to Town & Country magazine.

For the ninth season of Bravo’s reality series The Real Housewives of New York City, Mortimer became a cast member in 2017.

She introduced Pop Culture Living’s Home Collection, an online store with carefully chosen assortments of home accents, in 2014.

She presently sells her own housewares collection at Wayfair and Bed Bath & Beyond. Dinnerware, ornamental glasses, barware, and ice buckets are all part of the set.

With Winky Lux, Mortimer debuted a new line of artificial lashes in June 2019 under the name “XXO Tinsley for Winky Lux.” She was the one who created the product packaging.

During New York Fashion Week in September 2019, Mortimer participated in the Garo Sparo fashion show on the runway.

Mortimer revealed her collaboration with RuMe to debut her Resort 2020 Collection in October 2019. The collection features accessories for travel, fashion, and beauty.

Who Is Robert Bovard, Tinsley Mortimer Fiance?

Tinsley Mortimer’s fiance is Robert Bovard. He is a 40-year-old man who lives in Augusta, Georgia. Bovard is the President of Augusta Iron & Steel Works, Inc., where he’s worked for the past 12 years. He is a father of three and is reportedly a widower.
