Reddit Post On Stephen tWitch Boss Autopsy Photo

Publish date: 2024-09-17

There were posts shared on Reddit about Stephen tWitch’s autopsy photos.

Stephen tWitch was the former Ellen Degeneres Show DJ who recently passed away on December 13, 2022. 

His wife, Allison Holker Boss, confirmed that Stephen was 40 at the time of his death.

His wife shared a statement on December 14, 2022, “He was the backbone of our family, the best husband and father, and an inspiration to his fans.”

He was the father of three children, Weslie Renae, 14, Maddox Laurel, 6, and Zaia, 3.

According to sources, it was confirmed that Weslie was not the biological daughter of Stephen, but she was from her mother’s previous marriage.

After his death, the people who knew him shared social media posts where they said they would remember him in their prayers. 

Later, tWitch’s autopsy photo was rumored to be shared on Reddit. Let’s find out.

Stephen tWitch autopsy photo Reddit

According to sources, it was confirmed that there was no autopsy photo shared on the Reddit platform.

However, some people were searching on the internet as they assumed his autopsy photos would be shared on Reddit. 

However, when his dead body was found in an L.A. hotel room, it looked as if he shot himself with the gun. 

It was reported by the LAPD department that Stephen’s wife ran to them and told them that Stephen didn’t take his car with him while leaving his home. 

Stephen tWitch autopsy 

After his death, his dead body was taken for a medical examination. Later, the L.A. medical examiner ruled Stephen’s death to be suicide.
