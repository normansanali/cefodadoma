Ameno Amapiano Remix Song On Tiktok Find The Lyrics Meaning and English Translation

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Ameno Amapiano remix song has ammased a lot of attention on Tiktok. 

Premium Times mentioned that the song is created by Goya Menor and Nektunez. Goya penned the lyrics, sang, and rapped on the track. On the other hand, the Amapiano version of the song was produced by the US-based Ghanian-born Nektunez.

Amapiano, which originated on the streets of South Africa, is quickly becoming the world’s most famous sound.

Ameno Amapiano Remix Song Is Loved On Tiktok

Ameno Amapiano remix song is popular on Tiktok. People are making several kinds of videos using the song in the background.

Some can be seen making dance moves, whereas others have used the song to express their feelings during a particular situation. Moreover, the choir in the remix used behind is from an original music Ameno. Era’s song (stylized as +eRa+) is a French new-age musical project.

It was released as a single from their self-titled debut album Era in June 1996. It became a success in France, Belgium, Switzerland, Poland, and Latin America.

On the other hand, the remix has 1 million views on Youtube, whereas 775.8k videos have been made using this song on Tiktok.

The song has become a national anthem for the youths in many countries, especially Nigeria. The remix has catchy music in which people can vibe and make some dance moves. However, the original song gives a spiritual aura; thus, people cannot make any dance moves.

The creator of this track said that he did not create it, thinking it would be a big project. Instead, he unexpectedly got a lot of attention through his creation. Thus, it became a breakthrough for his career.

English Translation Of Ameno Amapiano Remix Song

The English translation of Ameno Amapiano is not available on the internet yet. However, the song itself uses some of the lines in English.

The choir in the background reads, “Ameno, Dori me interim Adapare. Dori me. Ameno Ameno. Latire, Latiremo.” This song has a spiritual meaning.

Its translation is “Disclose, Take me to the inside, absorb, take me. Disclose, disclose, hide, hiding oneself, take me. Disclose, unperceived signs, disclose. Tell me, tell me, warlike, spirit martyr, disclose”.
