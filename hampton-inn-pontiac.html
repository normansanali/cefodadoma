Hampton Inn - Pontiac | Enjoy Illinois

Publish date: 2024-09-25

Boasting a convenient location off of Interstate 55 (exit 197) and near historic Route 66, Hampton Inn Pontiac offers easy access to local attractions and restaurants.

When you're on a road trip, like traveling down Historic Route 66 - you'll get to experience some different and unique things. When it comes to where you sleep sometimes, the familiar comforts are the best. When you stay at the Hampton Inn in Pontiac, you get all the comforts and conveniences of this national Hotel chain.

The location is just off of Interstate 55, not too far away from the amazing Route 66 downtown that Pontiac is known for. Check out the Illinois Route 66 Hall of Fame and Pontiac Oakland Automobile Museum, and get a great meal at Edinger's Filling Station.

Some of the great benefits of staying at the Hampton Inn - Pontiac include: free hot breakfast buffet (6-10am), fitness center, indoor heated pool, business center, and 24-hour treats shop.
