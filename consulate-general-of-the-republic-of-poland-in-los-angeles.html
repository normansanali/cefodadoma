About Consulate General of the Republic of Poland in Los Angeles

Polish Consulate in Los Angeles runs an inclusive range of consular services to local, Polish, and international citizens in United-states.

The Polish Consulate in Los Angeles supports American citizens through its consular services, if they:

  • Need general or specific info regarding Polish economy, culture, sports, education and more
  • Need information regarding requirements and process of getting Polish Citizenship
  • Specific contacts and information in Poland
  • Want to apply and obtain Polish Visa

Visa and Passport services

Polish passport and visa from are issued only to citizens whose main residence is in Poland and United-states.

The process of issuing Polish Visa and Passport of Poland can take few weeks.

*Please contact with Polish Consulate in Los Angeles if you have any question as regards of visa and passport matters.

Polish representations in United-states

Polish representations worldwide

Foreign representations in United-states
