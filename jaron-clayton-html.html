Jaron Clayton - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Popular social media personality and YouTuber best known by his co-owned theblondebrewer TikTok account that he runs with his wife. They have gained popularity on the internet primarily from their short comedy videos, where they make skits about life as a married couple.

Before Fame

He and his wife first began creating content for TikTok in May of 2020. Their very first TikTok video was a lip-syncing video posted on May 6, 2020.

Trivia

He was once given NFL tickets to see the Seattle Seahawks in October of 2023. The Seahawks later won the game with a score of 20-10 over the Arizona Cardinals. Due to their popularity on TikTok, they have amassed over 5 million followers and 178 million likes on their profile.

Family Life

He lives along with his wife Maggie and their pet dog Chester. He is a native of Seattle, Washington. 

Associated With

He posted a November 14, 2023 comedy-themed TikTok video that featured the song "Monaco" by Bad Bunny.
