Beedrill (Japanese: スピアー Spear) is a Super-class Tretta. It is part of The Strongest in History! Mewtwo Max Voltage!! set.

Tretta data

Pokémon description
It flies around prey at great speed, stabbing with the poisonous barb on its rear.
