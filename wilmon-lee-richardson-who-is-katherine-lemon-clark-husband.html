Who is Katherine Lemon Clark husband?

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Catherine Lemon Clark is the mother of seasoned American broadcast journalist Don Lemon.

Lemon has been with CNN since 2006 and has risen through the ranks to be a news anchor and hosts his own show thus ‘Don Lemon Tonight’.

Lemon is one of the few openly gay black men in broadcast journalism since he came out to his mother 15 years ago.

His father was called Wilmon Lee Richardson.

Who is Katherine Lemon Clark’s husband Wilmon Lee Richardson?

Katherine and Wilmon were never legally bound (married) but they had Lemon out of a relationship they had.

Richardson was married to Annie Lawson while in a relationship with Katherine.

Katherine was also married but later got divorced.

Lemon was given the surname of his stepfather and only discovered at the age of five that Richardson was his biological father.

Lemon revealed that after the discovery there was a great relationship between them as father and son.

Richardson was an attorney and a World War II US Army veteran.

Lemon revealed that his father passed away when he was nine years old from complications as a result of diabetes.

Who are their children?

Don Lemon is the child of Lemon Clark and Richardson.
