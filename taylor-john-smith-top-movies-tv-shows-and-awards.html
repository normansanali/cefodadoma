Taylor John Smith top movies and TV shows

American actor Taylor John Smith. His most well-known performance was as John Keene in the television series Sharp Objects. He has starred in a number of noteworthy movies, including Wolves (2016), You Get Me (2017), Hunter Killer (2018), The Outpost (2020), Shadow in the Cloud (2020), and Where Crawdads Sing (2022).

In the When I Grow Up episode of Grey’s Anatomy from season 11, Taylor John Smith portrayed Nick. He was also in the Hunger Games.
