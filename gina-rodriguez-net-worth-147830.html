Gina Rodriguez is an American actress born in Chicago, IL on July 30, 1984. She is best known for her role as Jane the Virgin in the CW series of the same name, for which she won a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress in a TV Series, Musical or Comedy in 2015. She has also appeared in the CBS soap opera The Bold and the Beautiful and the 2012 musical movie Filly Brown.

About

After appearing as Beverly in seventeen episodes of the CBS soap opera The Bold and the Beautiful, she starred in the 2012 musical movie Filly Brown. In 2014, she was cast in the starring role in The CW series Jane the Virgin. The role earned her a 2015 Golden Globe Award for Best Actress in a TV Series, Musical or Comedy.

Before Fame

After training as a salsa Dancer during her childhood years, she studied theater at New York University's famous Tisch School of the Arts.

Trivia

She worked and trained at the Atlantic Theatre Company. 

Family Life

She and her two older sisters grew up in a Puerto Rican family in Chicago, Illinois. In 2016, she began dating model Joe LoCicero. 

Associated With

She was cast alongside Andrea Navedo, Yael Grobglas, and Justin Baldoni on Jane the Virgin.
