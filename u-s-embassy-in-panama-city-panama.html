About U.S. Embassy in Panama City, Panama

American Embassy in Panama City runs an inclusive range of consular services to local, American, and international citizens in Panama.

The American Embassy in Panama City supports Panamanian citizens through its consular services, if they:

  • Need general or specific info regarding American economy, culture, sports, education and more
  • Need information regarding requirements and process of getting American Citizenship
  • Specific contacts and information in United States
  • Want to apply and obtain American Visa

Visa and Passport services

American passport and visa from are issued only to citizens whose main residence is in United States and Panama.

The process of issuing American Visa and Passport of United States can take few weeks.

*Please contact with American Embassy in Panama City if you have any question as regards of visa and passport matters.

American representations in Panama

American Embassy in Panama City is the only American diplomatic mission in Panama.

    Panamanian representations in United States

    • Panamanian Embassy in Washington DC is the only Panamanian representation in United States

    American representations worldwide

    American Embassy In Panama City represents one of 257 consular and diplomatic representations of United States all over the world.

    Foreign representations in Panama

    American Embassy In Panama City represents one of 63 foreign consular and diplomatic representations from around the world in Panama.
