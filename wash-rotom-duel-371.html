Wash Rotom (Japanese: ウォッシュロトム Wash Rotom) is a figure in Pokémon Duel. It was made available in version 6.2.0.

Abilities

Sprinkler スプリンクラー
If this Pokémon is on the field and is not affected by a special condition, your Water-type Pokémon can MP move through other Water-type Pokémon.

Trivia

  • Prior to version 7.0.14, this figure was unable to be purchased in the Material Exchange.

Origin

This figure depicts the Wash Rotom form of Rotom. Discharge and Hydro Pump are moves in the Pokémon games that Rotom can learn, the latter upon changing into this form. Gigavolt Havoc and Hydro Vortex are Z-Moves in the games that Wash Rotom can use by equipping Electrium Z and Waterium Z, respectively.
