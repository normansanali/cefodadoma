Mark Hurd

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Mark Hurd was a technology executive from the United States who served as the CEO of three major technology businesses during his career. Hurd was a member of the board of directors and co-CEO of Oracle Corporation, a worldwide technology firm. Hurd, who rose to prominence as a business magnate and was a member of the Technology CEO Council and the board of directors of News Corporation, died on October 18, 2019.

After Larry Ellison, Hurd was Oracle’s second-highest-paid executive. As the Chief Executive Officer and Director of Oracle, Hurd used to earn a total salary of $108 million. Hurd had managed to live a wealthy and extravagant lifestyle with his million-dollar income.

Where is Mark Hurd From?

Mark Hurd was born in Manhattan, New York, in the United States, on January 1, 1957. Mark Vincent Hurd was Hurd’s real name. He was an American citizen. Hurd was of White ethnicity, and his zodiac sign was Capricon.

Hurd was nurtured in the heart of America, in New York, by his parents. Hurd was the son of debutante Teresa A. (mother) and financier Ralph Steiner Hurd (father). Hurd graduated in 1975 from Archbishop Curley-Notre Dame High School in Miami, Florida.

Hurd earned a Bachelor of Business Administration degree from Baylor University four years later, in 1979. With a tennis scholarship, he enrolled at Baylor University. Hurd also served on the “Baylor University Board of Regents,” “Phi Delta Theta,” and the Executive Committee as Vice-Chair.

What did Mark Hurd die of?

Mark Hurd, a 62-year-old multi-millionaire, died on Friday, October 18, 2019, after battling some health difficulties that have yet to be revealed. Hurd’s death has shocked his whole family, friends, and even the Oracle Corporation. Hurd has earlier stated that he would be on leave in September 2019 due to unexplained health difficulties.

Before he left, Hurd notified his co-CEO, Safra Catz, and Oracle’s founder, CTO Larry Ellison, that they would be handling the company’s activities in his absence. Hurd died a little over a month later from unidentified health problems.
