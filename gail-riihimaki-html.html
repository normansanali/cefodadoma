Gail Riihimaki - Trivia, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-09-21

Mother of the YouTube star LaurDIY. Lauren used makeup to make her mother look like her in a video posted on March 4, 2018. She and her husband are retired and formerly worked for General Motors. 

Before Fame

Her daughter began posting on YouTube on April 14, 2013, with a video in which she drew representations of certain stages of her life. 

Trivia

Her parents were interned in British Colombia during World War II. She is of Japanese descent. Her daughter's YouTube channel has over 8 million followers. 

Family Life

Her husband is Finnish and Ukrainian. Lauren is her daughter. 

Associated With

Her daughter began dating Jeremy Lewis.
