Who is Alana Blanchard?

Alana Blanchard is an American professional surfer and model. She has surfed on the ASP World Tour, though she has indicated she may take a break from competitive surfing in 2015.

She also designs and models Rip Curl swimwear, particularly a line of wetsuits, especially for surfing.

How many siblings does Alana have?

Alana Blanchard was born on  March 5, 1990, in Kauai, Hawaii, United States. Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is Caucasian.

She has a birth name, Alana Rene Blanchard. Her father’s name is Holt Blanchard and her mother’s name is unknown.

She started learning surfing from her father in her childhood, at the age of 4. She has two brothers, Byron Blanchard and Dorian Blanchard.

Alana Blanchard: Professional Life and Career

Alana Blanchard began her career in 2005.  She became the champion at T&C Women’s Pipeline Championship.

She took first place in the shortboard category in the tournament. After that, she won many competitions which ranked her 10th in the world of surfing.

She has won The Women’s Pipeline Championships in Hawaii. She also claimed the gold in The Rip Curl Girls Festival Junior Pro in Spain.

Again in Hawaii, the Gorgeous Alana Blanchard stood in the first position to win The Roxy Pro Trails.  She also made The Billabong Pro Pre-Trials in Hookipa, Maui championship trophy.

After that Blanchard made her successive win in The Volcom Pufferfish Surf Series in Pinetrees, Kauai, and grabbed the next trophy.

After a very inspiring and successful career, Alana is expected to take a break from competitive surfing. She is a very popular international personality.
