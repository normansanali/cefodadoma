Chris Provenzano Died: A Pillar of the Mahopac Soccer Community

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Today, we mourn the tragic loss of Chris Provenzano, a beloved coach and esteemed board member of the Mahopac Soccer community. Chris’s sudden passing has left us all heartbroken. His passion for soccer, his dedication to nurturing young talent, and his service as a board member have left an irreplaceable void in our hearts and in the Mahopac Soccer community.

A Passionate Coach

Chris Provenzano was more than just a coach or a board member. He was a mentor, a friend, and a guiding light to many young athletes in the Mahopac community. For several years, he dedicated himself to Mahopac Soccer, serving not only as a dedicated board member but also as a mentor.

Chris’s love for soccer was undeniable and infectious. His passion for the sport was evident in every match he coached, every strategy he planned, and every player he trained. He was the head coach of the “Mayhem”, one of Mahopac’s travel teams45, and his leadership and dedication were instrumental in shaping the team’s success.

Remembering Chris Provenzano

Chris Provenzano’s impact extended far beyond the soccer field. He was a resident of Yorktown Heights, New York, and his influence and goodwill were felt throughout the community. As a coach to his children’s teams, he instilled in them and their teammates a love for the sport that mirrored his own.

His sudden passing is a loss not just for the Mahopac Soccer community, but for everyone who had the privilege to know him. Tributes have been pouring in from players, parents, fellow coaches, and friends, all expressing their deep sorrow at his passing and sharing fond memories of a man who was much more than a coach.

Supporting the Provenzano Family

In response to this tragic event, GoFundMe pages have been set up to support Chris’s family during this difficult time. One fund is dedicated to supporting Chris’s children, while the other will go towards funeral expenses and support for his wife, Chelsea.

As we grapple with this enormous loss, our thoughts and prayers are with Chris’s family. We encourage anyone who can to contribute to these funds as a way to honor Chris’s memory and help his family in their time of need.

Chris Provenzano’s legacy will live on in the hearts of all those he impacted, both on and off the field. He will be deeply missed.
