At the time of penning this article in Dec 2021, Reggie Devore is 35 years.

Reggie Devore Wife

Reggie Devore is a family man. He is married to his wife Sasha Devore. They shared their wedding vows on 9 February 2007. Together, they have a son named Reggie III who celebrates his birthday on 4 July.

In April 2015, Reggie wrote on his Facebook, “This pic says so much…never behind but alway by my side. Thanks for having my back Sasha Devore. We have been together so long and we’re about to start a whole new chapter of our lives. I’m so excited about our future, glad to still be doing it with you!!! #dadjokes shout out to Kahli Jae for the photo.”

Talking about his wife, Sasha celebrates her birthday on 3 July every year. She is a Texas State University graduate who studied Applied Sociology with a minor in social work and graduate in 2012. She worked at Austin ISD.

Since April 2017, she has also been helping her husband as a booking manager.

Moreover, Sasha has also added her job at Manor ISD to her resume. Sasha was awarded by The Manor Independent School Board of Trustees as the teacher of the month at Lagos Elementary School in September 2021.

Besides her regular job, Sasha is also a writer who has published a book titled ‘Wake of Deception’ which was released on 2 December 2016. Sasha is also a musician who plays clarinet. In August 2019, Reggie shared a video of his wife teaching clarinet to their son.

Reggie Devore Net Worth

Reggie Devore has a net worth of $250 thousand. He is a percussionist turned rapper who has been working in the music industry and goes by the name DJ Reggie. Other than his DJing career, he has also been working as an audio engineer. Reggie’s social media pages are also filled with posts related to his music career.

When he is not spending time with his family, Reggie is mostly in the studio mastering his craft.

Reggie studied Audio Engineering at The Recording Conservatory of Austin. His Facebook revealed that he works at HeadBang Recordings and Blacklight Hip Hop. He has his own Blaklight Hip Hop website where you can check out his next events, book him, and purchase his merch.

Furthermore, the bio on his website states, “BlackLight is an American independent Hip Hop performing artist, audio engineer, and producer based in Austin, TX, whose personal brand of Hip Hop is a reflection of his daily slogan: “Make a way for yourself, and don’t let anyone slow down your hustle.” BlackLight creates refreshingly contemporary anthems, touching on socio-political themes as necessary.”

​BlackLight released his second album, titled “Full-Time,” in October of 2020 and performed at the OnePlus Music Festival in Mumbai, India as a special guest, opening for Dua Lipa and Katy Perry. Further accomplishments for BlackLight include featuring in a commercial and recent music video for OnePlus, a highlight in the February 2020 issue of EASTside Magazine, and an eight-page story in the June 2020 edition of Addition Magazine. He is currently recording for his third studio album. 

Outside of his music career, Reggie was in United States Air Force.
