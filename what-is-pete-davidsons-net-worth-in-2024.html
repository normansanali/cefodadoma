What is Pete Davidson's Net Worth in 2024?

Publish date: 2024-09-10

Pete Davidson, the renowned comedian and actor, has been making headlines not only for his comedic prowess but also for his skyrocketing net worth.

With his successful stints on television shows, movies, endorsements, and various other endeavors, Davidson has seen a substantial increase in his financial worth over the years.

Together with Fan Arch, we'll dissect Pete Davidson's net worth in 2024, shedding light on his earnings from different sources and providing a comprehensive analysis of his financial standing.

Earnings from Television Shows

Saturday Night Live

Pete Davidson's financial journey took off when he became a cast member on NBC's iconic sketch comedy show, Saturday Night Live (SNL), in 2014. During his tenure on the show, which spanned eight seasons until 2022, Davidson made significant earnings.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, his salary on SNL varied over the years. As a featured player in his first year, he earned approximately $7,000 per episode, which added up to around $147,000 per season.

His earnings increased to $15,000 per episode by the time he reached his fifth season, summing up to a substantial annual income.

While specific details of his salary towards the end of his SNL career remain unconfirmed, it's evident that his involvement with the show contributed significantly to his net worth.

Bupkis

In 2023, Davidson ventured into his own TV series, Bupkis, on NBC's streaming service Peacock. 

The show, based on Davidson's early life, featured him alongside prominent actors such as Edie Falco and Joe Pesci.

According to Variety, Davidson reportedly earned $500,000 per episode for Bupkis, which had an eight-episode first season and has been renewed for a second season. 

Earnings from Movies

Pete Davidson's foray into the world of cinema has been equally rewarding. From his early appearances in films like Trainwreck, What Men Want, and Set It Up, to his more significant roles in movies such as Big Time Adolescence, The Dirt, The Suicide Squad, and King of Staten Island, Davidson has seen a steady increase in his earnings from the film industry.

Notably, his involvement in the new movie, Dumb Money, which revolves around the GameStop craze of January 2021, has added to his financial success.

This film, alongside other acting gigs like Transformers: Rise of the Beasts, has contributed significantly to Davidson's net worth, showcasing his versatility and financial acumen in the world of entertainment.

Endorsements and Other Ventures

Apart from his television and film endeavors, Pete Davidson has also delved into endorsements and other ventures that have bolstered his financial worth.

While specific details of his earnings from endorsements remain private, it's evident that Davidson's status as a prominent public figure has allowed him to secure lucrative endorsement deals, further adding to his overall net worth.

Real Estate Investments

In addition to his earnings from the entertainment industry, Pete Davidson has made notable real estate investments that have contributed to his financial portfolio.

In 2021, Davidson purchased a $1.2 million condo in his hometown of Staten Island, NY, and subsequently listed it for sale at a higher price.

Furthermore, he bought a $1.3 million home for his mother on Staten Island, showcasing his inclination towards real estate investments.

Notably, his joint purchase, alongside SNL colleague Colin Jost, of a decommissioned Staten Island ferry for $280,100 stands as a unique and intriguing investment venture, reflecting Davidson's diverse financial portfolio.

 

On the whole, Pete Davidson's net worth in 2024 stands as a testament to his multifaceted success in the entertainment industry and astute financial decisions.

From his earnings from television shows, movies, endorsements, and real estate investments, Davidson's financial journey has been marked by substantial growth and strategic financial planning. 

Pete Davidson's financial journey serves as an inspiration, demonstrating how talent, hard work, and strategic financial decisions can culminate in remarkable financial success in the world of entertainment.
