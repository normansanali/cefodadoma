Natasha Richardson News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Name:Natasha Richardson

Hometown:Marylebone, UK

Birthday:May 11, 1963

About

Natasha Richardson was born in May 1963 in London to film producer and director Tony Richardson and legendary screen actress Vanessa Redgrave.

She married Irish actor Liam Neelson in 1994. Together the couple have two sons.

Richardson has stared in films like The Parent Trap with Dennis Quaid and Lindsay Lohan, Maid in Manhattan with Jennifer Lopez and Evening with Meryl Streep, Claire Danes and Toni Collette.

The actress died at age 45 in March 2009 of internal brain bleeding after suffering a head injury during a skiing accident in Canada.
