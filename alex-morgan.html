Alex Morgan is a professional soccer player. Alex Morgan had come to fame for being a member of the Orlando Pride in the National Women’s Soccer League (NWSL). As well as in the United States national team.

Early Life and Childhood

The player was born on July 2, 1989. Her parents are Pamela and Michael Morgan. The full name of Alex Morgan is Alexandra Morgan Carrasco and her birth name is Alexandra Patricia Morgan.

Her zodiac sign is cancer. Likewise, she was born in San Dimas, California. Presently, she is 34 years old. Her nationality and ethnicity are American.

Similarly, she has two siblings named Jeni and Jeri. Along with her two siblings, the family was shifted to the nearby suburb of Diamond Bar. Moreover, Diamon Bar is located about thirty miles east of Los Angeles.

From an early age, she used to be a multi-sport athlete. She had even begun playing soccer with the American Youth Soccer Organization (AYSC).

However, according to some sources, she didn’t play club soccer until she was 14 when she joined Cypress Elite. She was always interested and active in the sports field.

Education

Morgan completed high school at Diamond Bar High School. In her high school, she was a three-time all-league pick. And was named All-American by the National Soccer Coaches Association of America (NSCAA).

She was well-known for her speed and sprinting ability during that time.

At the age of 17, she was called up to the United States under-20 women’s national soccer team. And she got injured in an anterior cruciate ligament (ACL).

Moreover, she was playing in a scrimmage against the men’s junior national team. Because of this, she did not play for the team until April 2008.
